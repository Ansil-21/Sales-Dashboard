# Sales-Dashboard
## Vrinda Store Annual Report 2022
This project contains a complete Excel-based data analysis and interactive dashboard created using the sales dataset of Vrinda Store for the year 2022.
The dashboard has been designed to analyze customer purchase patterns, sales performance, order status, and geographical contribution to help improve business decisions and target marketing strategies.

## Objective of the Project
To study sales performance and consumer behavior and answer business-driven analytical questions such as:

-Which month generated the highest sales and orders?

-Who purchased more — men or women in 2022?

-Which state contributed the most to revenue?

-What are the different order statuses and their performance?

-Which sales channel contributed the maximum?

-Relation between age group and gender purchase frequency?

-What is the highest selling category?

-Compare sales vs orders on a single visual chart.

-List the Top 10 states contributing to the sales.

-Identify purchasing trends across demographics.

## Insights Identified

✔ Women buyers contribute more than men

✔ Most customers belong to adult age group

✔ Amazon, Ajio & Myntra bring majority of sales

✔ Certain states heavily dominate purchase volume

✔ Seasonal fluctuation visible across months

✔ Some order statuses require business attention (Returns/Cancellations)
